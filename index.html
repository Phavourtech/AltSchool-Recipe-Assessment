Jollof Rice Recipe

Jollof rice is a very popular West African dish made by cooking rice in a rich,spicy tomato based sauce.
It's loved in Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal, and many other African Countries, but Nigerian Jollof is famous for its smoky flavour and bold spices
This recipe shows a simple way to prepare delicious Jollof Rice at home.

Jollof Rice Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 25 minutes
  • Cook Time: 45 minutes
  • Servings: 5 people
  • Difficulty: Medium

Jollof Rice Ingredients

  • 4 cups of Basmati Rice
  • Gino Tomatoes Paste
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Seasoning Cubes
  • Salt & spices

Jollof Rice Cooking instructions

  1. Blend Tomatoes and pepper.
  2. Heat oil and fry the paste.
  3. Add stock, salt and seasoning.
  4. Pour in rice and cook until soft.
  5. Stir and allow to steam.

    6. Tips

    For extra Flavour: Add bay leaves, smoked fish or cray fish
